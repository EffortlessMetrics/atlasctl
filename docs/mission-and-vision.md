# Mission and vision

## Mission

Turn a repo’s behavior, proof surfaces, fixtures, commands, artifacts, and explanations into a deterministic atlas.

## Vision

A serious repo should answer:

- what behavior matters?
- what proves it?
- what fixture models it?
- what artifact should I inspect?
- what docs explain the trade-off?

`atlasctl` is the teaching layer for that.
